title: "Open when you need a hug"
date: "Written with love"
emoji: "🤗"
description: "For when you need to feel held"
---

Come here.

That's it. Let me hold you.

I know I can't physically wrap my arms around you right now, but I want you to close your eyes and imagine it. Picture me standing right in front of you.

My arms open wide. You step into them. And I pull you close.

Feel my chest against yours. My arms around your back, holding you tight. My chin resting on your head or your shoulder. My heartbeat, steady and calm, against you.

I'm holding you. Right now. In this moment.

Let everything else fade away. Just you and me, in this hug that spans any distance.

Stay here as long as you need. I'm not going anywhere.

Breathe me in. Let my presence, even imagined, ground you. Let my love surround you like a warm blanket on a cold day.

You are safe here. You are loved here. You are held.

I know it's not the same as really being in my arms. But I hope it helps, even a little.

And I promise, the next time I see you, I'll give you the longest, tightest, most real hug. One that makes up for every hug we've missed.

Until then, this virtual one will have to do. And it's yours. Whenever you need it. As many times as you need it.

I've got you. Always.

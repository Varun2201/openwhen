Come here.

That's it. Let me hold you for a while.

No words. No fixing things. Just me holding you, letting you know that you don't have to carry everything on your own.

Stay here for as long as you need. I'm not going anywhere.

I can't wait for the day I can give you this hug for real. Until then, I'm sending you the biggest virtual one.

I've got you. Always.
